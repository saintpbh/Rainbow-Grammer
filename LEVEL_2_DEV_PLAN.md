# Level 2 Development Plan: Time & Voice (Tense & Passive)

## Concept: "중간 매운맛" - 시제와 태의 변주
Level 2 introduces the concept of **Time (Tenses)** and **Perspective (Voice)**.
Sentences become more precise about *when* things happen and *to whom* things happen.

## Curriculum Overview

### Week 1: The Past & Future (과거와 미래)
Focus: `Simple Past (ed/irregular)` & `Future (will)`
- Day 1: Simple Past (Regular) - "I watched a movie." (나는 영화를 봤다.)
- Day 2: Simple Past (Irregular) - "He ate pizza." (그는 피자를 먹었다.)
- Day 3: Future with Will - "She will study hard." (그녀는 열심히 공부할 것이다.)
- Day 4: Future Questions - "Will you go?" (너는 갈 거니?)
- Day 5: Past Negatives - "He did not cry." (그는 울지 않았다.)
- Day 6: Comparison (Past vs Present) - "I ran, but now I walk."
- Day 7: Review Game

### Week 2: Continuous & Perfect (진행과 완료)
Focus: `Progressive (be -ing)` & `Perfect (have p.p)`
- Day 8: Past Continuous - "I was sleeping." (나는 자고 있었다.)
- Day 9: Present Perfect (Experience) - "I have seen a lion." (나는 사자를 본 적이 있다.)
- Day 10: Present Perfect (Just) - "She has just arrived." (그녀는 막 도착했다.)
- Day 11: Future Continuous - "He will be running." (그는 달리고 있을 것이다.)
- Day 12: Action Duration - "I have lived here for 10 years."
- Day 13: State vs Action - "I know him." (O) vs "I am knowing him." (X)
- Day 14: Review Game

### Week 3: Passive Voice (수동태)
Focus: `be + p.p` (Subject receives action)
- Day 15: Introduction - "The window was broken." (창문이 깨졌다.)
- Day 16: By whom? - "It was made by mom." (그것은 엄마에 의해 만들어졌다.)
- Day 17: Present Passive - "English is spoken here." (영어는 여기서 사용된다.)
- Day 18: Unknown Actor - "My bag was stolen." (내 가방이 도난당했다.)
- Day 19: Passive Questions - "When was it built?" (언제 지어졌니?)
- Day 20: Famous Inventions - "The light was invented by Edison."
- Day 21: Review Game

### Week 4: Helping Verbs (조동사)
Focus: `Can, Should, Must, May`
- Day 22: Ability (Can/Could) - "I can swim fast."
- Day 23: Advice (Should) - "You should sleep early."
- Day 24: Obligation (Must/Have to) - "He must go now."
- Day 25: Possibility (May/Might) - "It may rain soon."
- Day 26: Polite Requests - "Would you help me?"
- Day 27: My Dream (Using Modals) - "I must study to be a doctor."
- Day 28: Final Boss (Level 2 Graduation)
