# Level 3 Development Plan: Connecting Worlds (Conjunctions & Complex Sentences)

## Concept: "아주 매운맛" - 문장과 문장의 결합
Level 3 introduces **Conjunctions** and **Complex Sentences**.
Users learn to connect ideas, express reasons, conditions, and relative relationships. The sentences become "Longer and Richer".

## Curriculum Overview

### Week 1: Basic Connections (기본 접속사)
Focus: `And, But, Or, So` (Coordinating Conjunctions)
- Day 1: And (Adding) - "I like apples and he likes bananas."
- Day 2: But (Contrast) - "It is cold but I am happy."
- Day 3: Or (Choice) - "Do you want tea or coffee?"
- Day 4: So (Result) - "It rained, so we stayed home."
- Day 5: Compound Subjects/Objects - "Tom and Jerry ran fast."
- Day 6: Short Stories - Mixing and/but/so.
- Day 7: Review Game

### Week 2: Logic & Time (종속 접속사 1)
Focus: `Because, When, Before, After`
- Day 8: Reason (Because) - "I cried because I was sad."
- Day 9: Time (When) - "When I wake up, I drink water."
- Day 10: Sequence (Before) - "Wash hands before you eat."
- Day 11: Sequence (After) - "We played soccer after school."
- Day 12: While (Simultaneous) - "He sang while he cooked."
- Day 13: Logic Puzzle - cause and effect connection.
- Day 14: Review Game

### Week 3: Possibilities (가정법 맛보기)
Focus: `If` (Conditionals)
- Day 15: Real Conditional - "If it rains, I will stay home."
- Day 16: Zero Conditional - "If you heat ice, it melts."
- Day 17: Requests - "If you are busy, call me later."
- Day 18: Advice - "If you are sick, go to a doctor."
- Day 19: Unless (If not) - "Unless you run, you will receive late."
- Day 20: Dreams (If I were..) - "If I were a bird, I could fly."
- Day 21: Review Game

### Week 4: The Relatives (관계대명사)
Focus: `Who, Which, That` (Relative Clauses)
- Day 22: People (Who) - "I know the man who is singing."
- Day 23: Things (Which) - "This is the cake which she made."
- Day 24: That (Universal) - "The book that I read is fun."
- Day 25: Whose (Possession) - "I have a friend whose father is a pilot."
- Day 26: Places (Where) - "This is the park where we met."
- Day 27: Sophisticated Speech - "The girl who lives next door is my friend."
- Day 28: Final Boss (Level 3 Graduation)
