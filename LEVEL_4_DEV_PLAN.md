# Level 4 Development Plan: Native Nuance (Idioms, Phrasal Verbs & Advanced Structure)

## Concept: "불닭맛" - 원어민처럼 말하기
Level 4 moves beyond literal translation. It focuses on **Phrasal Verbs**, **Idioms**, and **Advanced Structures** (like Inversion) that make English sound natural and sophisticated.

## Curriculum Overview

### Week 1: Phrasal Verbs (구동사)
Focus: `Verb + Preposition` with changed meaning.
- Day 1: Daily Life (Get up, Turn on) - "I get up early and turn on the radio."
- Day 2: Movement (Get in, Get out) - "Get in the car quickly."
- Day 3: Relationships (Get along, Break up) - "They get along well."
- Day 4: Searching & Solving (Look for, Figure out) - "I am looking for my keys."
- Day 5: Endurance (Put up with, Give up) - "Never give up your dream."
- Day 6: Care (Look after, Take care of) - "She looks after her sister."
- Day 7: Review Game

### Week 2: Idioms & Expressions (관용구)
Focus: Colorful language.
- Day 8: Weather Idioms - "It's raining cats and dogs."
- Day 9: Body Idioms - "Keep your chin up."
- Day 10: Food Idioms - "It is a piece of cake."
- Day 11: Animal Idioms - "Hold your horses."
- Day 12: Color Idioms - "I feel blue today."
- Day 13: Time Idioms - "Better late than never."
- Day 14: Review Game

### Week 3: Advanced Structure I (To-Infinitives & Gerunds)
Focus: `To V` vs `V-ing` as Objects/Complements.
- Day 15: Want to vs Enjoy ing - "I want to go, but I enjoy staying."
- Day 16: Stop to vs Stop ing - "He stopped to smoke." vs "He stopped smoking."
- Day 17: To be honest (Phrase) - "To be honest, I am tired."
- Day 18: Too ~ to - "It is too hot to go out."
- Day 19: Enough to - "He is rich enough to buy a car."
- Day 20: It takes time - "It takes two hours to fly there."
- Day 21: Review Game

### Week 4: Advanced Structure II (Emphasis & Inversion)
Focus: `Inversion` and emphasizing parts of sentences.
- Day 22: Never have I... (Inversion) - "Never have I seen such a huge pie."
- Day 23: Here comes... - "Here comes the bus!"
- Day 24: Not only... but also - "He is not only smart but also kind."
- Day 25: It is ~ that (Cleft) - "It is Tom that broke the window."
- Day 26: Comparatives (The more, the better) - "The more you read, the smarter you get."
- Day 27: Speech Level - "Ladies and gentlemen, allow me to introduce him."
- Day 28: Final Boss (Level 4 Graduation)
