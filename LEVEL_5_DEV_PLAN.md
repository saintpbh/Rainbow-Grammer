# Level 5 Development Plan: Master Class (News, Speeches & Literature)

## Concept: "핵불닭맛" - 영어의 정점
Level 5 is the final boss. It uses **real-world complex English** found in News Headlines, Famous Speeches, Literature, and Philosophy.
The goal is to handle abstract concepts, formal tone, and poetic expressions.

## Curriculum Overview

### Week 1: Headlines & News (시사 영어)
Focus: `Compressed Grammar` (Headlines often drop be-verbs/articles).
- Day 1: Economy - "Stock market crashes as prices rise."
- Day 2: Technology - "AI replaces human workers in factories."
- Day 3: Environment - "Global warming threatens polar bear habitats."
- Day 4: Politics - "President announces new tax policy today."
- Day 5: Science - "NASA discovers water on distant planet."
- Day 6: Sports - "Underdog team defeats champion in finals."
- Day 7: Review Game

### Week 2: Famous Speeches (연설문)
Focus: `Rhetoric & Rhythm` (Formal & Persuasive).
- Day 8: Steve Jobs - "Stay hungry, stay foolish."
- Day 9: MLK Jr. - "I have a dream that one day..."
- Day 10: Lincoln - "Government of the people, by the people."
- Day 11: Churchill - "We shall never surrender."
- Day 12: Gandhi - "Be the change you wish to see."
- Day 13: JFK - "Ask not what your country can do."
- Day 14: Review Game

### Week 3: Literature & Wisdom (문학과 철학)
Focus: `Poetic & Abstract` language.
- Day 15: Shakespeare - "To be or not to be, that is the question."
- Day 16: Proverbs - "A journey of a thousand miles begins with a step."
- Day 17: Philosophy (Descartes) - "I think, therefore I am."
- Day 18: Little Prince - "What is essential is invisible to the eye."
- Day 19: Poetry (Frost) - "I took the one less traveled by."
- Day 20: Modern Wisdom - "Life is what happens when you are making plans."
- Day 21: Review Game

### Week 4: The Final Challenge (종합 완성)
Focus: `Mastery Test` across all genres.
- Day 22: Complex News Analysis
- Day 23: Academic Abstract (Science/History)
- Day 24: Business Correspondance (Formal Email)
- Day 25: Debate Argument (Pros/Cons)
- Day 26: Narrative Storytelling (Advanced)
- Day 27: Reflection (My Rainbow Journey)
- Day 28: GRAND FINALE (Ending Credits)
